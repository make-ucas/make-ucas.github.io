Ke Ma

Associate Professor (Tenure-track Assistant Professor) School of Electronic, Electrical and Communication Engineering, UCAS.

Hi! I’m Ke Ma(马坷), an Associate Professor (Tenure-track Assistant Professor) with the School of Electronic, Electrical and Communication Engineering, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. I got my Ph.D. degree from Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IIE, CAS) in 2019, supervised by Prof. Xiaochun Cao (Professor at Sun Yat-sen University, Shenzhen Compus, China). I am also lucky to have opportunities to collaborate with Qingming Huang (IEEE Fellow), Qianqian Xu (Professor at ICT CAS), Yuan Yao (Professor at HKUST), Jiechao Xiong (Research Fellow at Tencent Lab AI Lab), Zhiyong Yang (Tenure-track Assistant Professor at UCAS), Yangbangyan Jiang (Postdoc at UCAS), Xiaojun Jia (Research Fellow at NTU), and Siyuan Liang (Research Fellow at NUS).

I’m especially interested in several fundamental problems in machine learning:

  • Adversarial Learning
  • Robust Optimization
  • Robust Game Theory

I’m always looking for highly self-motivated PhD/Master students!
